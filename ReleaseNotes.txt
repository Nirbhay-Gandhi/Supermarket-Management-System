#Release notes
Major.Minor.Patch:
-------------------
Major: Significant, usually backward-incompatible changes.
Minor: New features or enhancements that are backward-compatible.
Patch: Bug fixes or minor improvements that are backward-compatible.
For example, version 2.5.1 indicates a major version 2, minor version 5, and patch version 1
